Our Origin

The Story Behind Nzoka Kivuva Foundation

In grassroots communities, thousands of young talents stall due to a lack of safe facilities, structured sports mentorship, and financial pathways to remain in school. Witnessing these systemic gaps firsthand sparked a mission to create a lawful, highly structured platform capable of bridging social divides and fostering national cohesion through sports.

The Nzoka Kivuva Foundation was established to transform sports from a casual recreational activity into a powerful, institutional tool for health, discipline, teamwork, and personal development. By tying athletic participation directly to academic excellence and life skills training, we ensure youth don't just excel on the pitch — they secure their future in the classroom and beyond.

Today, the foundation operates with a clear mandate: to nurture talent, provide educational equity, and build resilient communities from the ground up — all while maintaining absolute transparency and accountability in every program we deliver. Partner with us to make a difference →

Meet the Nzoka Kivuva Foundation Board of Directors

Guiding our mission with expertise, integrity, and unwavering commitment.

Advocate Nzoka Kivuva
Executive Director & Founder

A practicing legal professional who oversees the strategic vision, resource mobilization, and legal compliance of the foundation. He ensures that all institutional activities align perfectly with national policy, safeguarding transparency, child protection, and corporate governance across all field initiatives in Machakos County and beyond.

Sau Sanya
Director of Grassroots Programs & Sports Development

An expert in sports administration responsible for planning local tournaments, structural outreach, and community club partnerships. He directs the implementation of leagues across Machakos County, focusing on integrating life skills, discipline, and anti-discrimination frameworks into youth coaching and competitive events.

Elizabeth Kalekye
Director of Educational Equity & Youth Mentorship

A seasoned educational consultant who manages the foundation's sports-linked scholarship funds, vocational training pipelines, and academic materials distribution. She evaluates school partnerships and structures mentorship programs aimed at boosting the marketability of youth skills and promoting educational excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions About Nzoka Kivuva Foundation

Everything you need to know about our organization, programs, and how to get involved.

Nzoka Kivuva Foundation is a registered Kenyan nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering young people through sports, education, leadership development, and community transformation across Machakos County and Kenya.

Our administrative headquarters is in Kitengela, but our field operations are based in Machakos County. We also work with partners across Kenya to expand our reach and impact.
