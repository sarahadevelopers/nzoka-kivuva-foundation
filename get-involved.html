Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting involved.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes. Nzoka Kivuva Foundation is a registered entity (CLG-BWTORQPV). Tax receipts are available on request for all donations.

How are scholarships awarded?

Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance, sports achievement, and demonstrated need. Applications are reviewed by our scholarship committee.

Can I volunteer from outside Machakos?

Yes. We welcome volunteers from across Kenya and beyond. Virtual volunteering opportunities are also available for specific roles.

How do schools register?

Schools can register through the "Sports Club" tab above or by contacting us directly. We partner with schools to host tournaments and offer scholarships.

Can companies sponsor tournaments?

Absolutely. We offer sponsorship packages for tournaments, training programs, and community events. Contact us for a tailored proposal.
