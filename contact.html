Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about connecting with Nzoka Kivuva Foundation.

You can apply to volunteer through our Get Involved page. We welcome volunteers with skills in coaching, teaching, mentorship, event organization, administration, and more.

We welcome partnerships with schools, corporate organizations, county governments, sports bodies, and community groups. Contact us via our form or email us at info@nzokakivuvafoundation.org to discuss partnership opportunities.

Our administrative headquarters is located at Top Doctors Plaza, 4th Floor, Old Namanga Road, Kitengela, Kenya. Our field operations are based in Machakos County.

Students can apply for sports-linked scholarships through our Get Involved page. Applications are open to students in Machakos County who excel in both academics and sports.

You can donate through our Get Involved page via M-Pesa Paybill, bank transfer, or contact us directly for other payment options. Every contribution helps us empower more young people in Kenya.
