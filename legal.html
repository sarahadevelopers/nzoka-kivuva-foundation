Compliance & Accountability

Transparency is our foundation.

We operate with full legal compliance and institutional integrity, ensuring every resource is managed responsibly and every program is delivered with accountability.

Registration & Legal Status

The Nzoka Kivuva Foundation is a fully registered, legally constituted public benefit organization operating in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Kenya. Our legal identity ensures that all partnerships, donations, and program implementations are conducted within a framework of institutional integrity and regulatory compliance.

  • Registration Number: CLG-BWTORQPV
  • Legal Status: Registered Public Benefit Organization
  • Jurisdiction: Republic of Kenya
  • Operational Areas: Machakos County (Field Operations) & Kitengela (Head Office)

Safeguarding & Child Protection Policy

The Nzoka Kivuva Foundation is unwavering in its commitment to safeguarding the rights, dignity, and well-being of every child and vulnerable person we serve. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards any form of abuse, discrimination, or exploitation.

Our Safeguarding Commitments:

  • All staff, volunteers, and partners undergo rigorous child protection training and background checks.
  • We promote gender equality and anti-discrimination in all our sports and education programs.
  • Safe, inclusive, and accessible environments are maintained for all participants, especially vulnerable and underserved groups.
  • We have clear, confidential reporting channels for any safeguarding concerns.
  • Our programs integrate values of respect, fair play, and conflict resolution as core principles.

Transparency & Accountability

We believe that trust is built through openness. The Nzoka Kivuva Foundation operates with a rigorous accountability framework that ensures every resource—whether financial, human, or material—is deployed effectively and ethically to achieve measurable community impact.

  • Financial Transparency: All funds are meticulously tracked and audited, with regular reporting to donors and stakeholders.
  • Program Monitoring: We continuously evaluate our programs to ensure they meet their objectives and adapt to community needs.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: We actively involve community members, county governments, and partners in decision-making processes.
  • Public Reporting: Annual reports and financial statements are made publicly available for review.
