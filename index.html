Youth Empowerment

1,200+ young people engaged in sports and physical activities annually.

Through grassroots sports programs, we promote physical activity as a tool for health, discipline, and teamwork. Our initiatives help reduce youth vulnerability to health risks while building lifelong habits of wellness.

  • ✅ 48 community sports facilities supported
  • ✅ 156 tournaments organized across Machakos
  • ✅ 1,200+ youth participating in structured programs

Education
02

Educational Equity

320+ sports-linked scholarships awarded to deserving students.

We believe education opens doors. By linking sports participation to academic excellence, we provide scholarships, learning materials, and vocational training to young people who might otherwise be left behind.

  • ✅ 48 partner schools and community clubs
  • ✅ 320 scholarships funded to date
  • ✅ Learning materials distributed across 12 schools
Learn more about our education programs →
Leadership
03

Youth Leadership

500+ young leaders mentored through our programs.

Leadership is cultivated through mentorship, life skills training, and positive role modeling. Our programs create platforms that nurture talent, innovation, and social responsibility, preparing young people to become leaders in their communities.

  • ✅ 32 coaches certified in leadership development
  • ✅ 8 mentorship programs running annually
  • ✅ Life skills training integrated into all programs

We support vulnerable groups through health initiatives, civic participation platforms, and sustainable development solutions. By strengthening communities, we create lasting change that benefits everyone.

  • ✅ Social halls for civic education established
  • ✅ Vulnerable groups supported with health initiatives
